About Volticle

Our story at Volticle, a premier Electric Two-Wheeler Repair Centre, started on July 16, 2019. On that day, we took on a challenge and became the first team in Kerala to successfully repair an Ather 450 without any external support. This achievement marked the beginning of our mission to become specialists in electric vehicle maintenance.

Today, our expertise extends to a wide range of electric two-wheelers, including OLA, Simple One, and Hero Vida models. We are committed to delivering work that meets the highest factory standards. We also believe in complete transparency, allowing you to witness the repair process on your vehicle firsthand.
